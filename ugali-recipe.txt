Ugali Recipe

Ingredients
- 4 cups Water
- 2 cups corn flour
- 2 tbsp butter

Directions
1. Bring water to a boil in a pot and add butter
2. Gradually add corn flour into the boiling water while stirring with a wooden stick
3. Reduce heat to low and continue mixing the flour until all the mixture has blended 
4. Shape the mixture into cone form
5. Cover and let it sit for 10 minutes on low heat
6. Empty the corn meal into a plate and shape it as desired
7. Serve with vegetables or meat stew
